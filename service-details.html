Valuation under Insolvency & Bankruptcy Code, 2016

Our valuation services under the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Code (IBC), 2016 are designed to provide accurate and compliant valuations of assets during insolvency proceedings. We assist creditors, debtors, resolution professionals, and adjudicating authorities with comprehensive valuations to facilitate fair recovery and equitable distribution of assets.

We follow the standards prescribed by the IBBI (Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India) and conduct thorough due diligence to assess the fair market value of assets. Our experienced valuers provide detailed reports that support the resolution process and ensure transparency in asset recovery proceedings.

Why Choose Our IBC Valuation Services

Our valuers are well-versed in IBC requirements and provide valuations that are accepted by courts and regulatory authorities.

  • IBBI Compliant Valuations
  • Fair Market Value Assessment
  • Transparent & Detailed Reports
  • Expert Legal & Technical Support

We provide valuation services across various asset classes including real estate, plant and machinery, intangible assets, and inventories. Our comprehensive approach ensures that all stakeholders have a clear understanding of asset values during the corporate insolvency resolution process, promoting fair and transparent asset distribution.

What Methods Do You Use for IBC Valuations?
We employ multiple valuation methods including the Income Approach, Market Approach, and Cost Approach. The selection depends on the asset type and available data. Our approach strictly adheres to the Valuation Standards prescribed by the IBBI.
How Long Does an IBC Valuation Take?
The timeline depends on the complexity and scope of the valuation. Typically, an IBC valuation takes 2-4 weeks from data collection to final report delivery. Urgent valuations can be expedited upon request.
Are Your Valuations Court-Approved?
Yes, our valuations are prepared in strict accordance with IBBI standards and are admissible in courts. Our qualified valuers are registered with relevant regulatory bodies ensuring credibility and acceptance by judicial authorities.
What Asset Classes Can You Value Under IBC?
We can value a wide range of assets including real estate, plant & machinery, inventories, financial assets, intellectual property, and goodwill. Our multidisciplinary team ensures comprehensive coverage across all asset categories.
